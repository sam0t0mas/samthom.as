Project documentation outlining the delivery logistics tracking from Amazon, hardware unboxing, and execution initialization of VMware Cloud Foundation 9.x (VCF 9.x) within the decentralized mini-PC cluster infrastructure.

Delivery manifested via Amazon Premium Fulfillment. The parcel consists of mission-critical compute elements, low-latency interconnect storage upgrades, and high-density node components necessary to build the unified Management and Workload domains required by the VCF 9 architectural framework.
